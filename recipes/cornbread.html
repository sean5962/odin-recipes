How to Make Cornbread

Whisk the dry ingredients in a large bowl, then add the wet ingredients and whisk until well-combined. Pour in a prepared cake pan and bake in a preheated oven until a toothpick comes out clean.

Cornbread is great with just about anything, but it pairs especially well with hearty soups, stews, and chilis.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup yellow cornmeal
  • ⅔ cup white sugar
  • 3 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • ⅓ cup vegetable oil

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9-inch round cake pan.
  2. Whisk flour, cornmeal, sugar, baking powder, and salt together in a large bowl. Add milk, vegetable oil, and egg; whisk until well combined. Pour batter into the prepared pan.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of the pan comes out clean, 20 to 25 minutes.
